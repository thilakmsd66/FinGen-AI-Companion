Corporate Actions: Understanding Their Impact and Role of Custodians
Corporate actions refer to events or decisions made by a company that affect its securities (stocks, bonds, etc.) and can have a significant impact on shareholders, bondholders, and other stakeholders. These actions are typically carried out by the company’s board of directors or management and can result in changes to the company’s structure, financial standing, or distribution of profits.
